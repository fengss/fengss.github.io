    环境

    • gateway.songxiaocai.org 联调

    登录

    Method:Post

    http://gateway.songxiaocai.org/oauth/login

    请求参数

    Content-Type:Application/Json

    根据loginType的类型,组装具体的json请求数据

    1:钉钉H5登录, 2:钉钉Pc/App登录,3:微信H5,4:微信小程序,5:手机号密码, 6:手机号短信验证码

    类型 数据格式 备注
    通用 {“deviceInfoVO”:{“deviceType”:3,”clientVersion”:”xxx”,”clientSysVersion”:”xxx”,”clientSysName”:”xxx”}, xxx:……} deviceType为设备类型1:”iOS”,2:”Android”,3:”PC”,4:”h5”;clientVersion为设备版本号 clientSysName为设备名举例:clientSysName: chrome、clientSysVersion:84.0.4147、clientVersion: OS X 10.12
    1,2 {“appKey”:11,”loginType”:2,”dtCode”:”a56094ebd02b3a6f87837c679c3701d3”} appKey为应用唯一标识loginType指定当前的登录dtCode为钉钉身份码
    5 {“appKey”:11,”loginType”:5,”mobilePhone”:19988880111,”password”:12345,”captchaId“:”123123”,”captcha”:”1111”} appKey为应用唯一标识loginType指定当前的登录mobilePhone为手机号password为明文密码captchaId为图形验证码ID(可不填)captcha为图形验证码内容(可不填)
    6 {“appKey”:11,”loginType”:5,”mobilePhone”:19988880111,”smsCaptcha**“:”123123”}** appKey为应用唯一标识loginType指定当前的登录mobilePhone为手机号smsCaptcha短信验证码
